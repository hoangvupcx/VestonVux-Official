THE QUEEN’S GAMBIT

Thirty-two pieces, sixty-four squares, limitless possibilities. The art of chess is the perfect metaphor for the VesstonVux Made to Measure service. A work of skill and calculated precision that brings to life timeless pieces, perfectly embodying the brand’s unique blend of contemporary and classic.

MODERNITY MEETS TRADITION

The Made to Measure services combines the longstanding Italian sartorial know-how with the innovation of the VestonVux design studios. The result has the refinement of the brand's signature aesthetics and the exquisite precision and level of personalisation that only bespoke suiting can offer. The highest expression of quality and design.

A SUIT FULL OF CHARACTER

Presence, style, charm: the Made to Measure service embodies the hallmarks of VestonVux’s style

Created with individual changes to make them comfortable and practical, our suits work well not only for special occasions but also for a dynamic lifestyle

To meet the needs of a sophisticated, demanding clientele who want their clothes to truly reflect and enhance their strong personality.


Starting with the fitting, exact measurements are carefully taken, ensuring an impeccable fit.

There begins an entire process of customisation, personalisation and dedication.

Each of our customers is guided through the process of constructing a garment that is expertly tailored especially for them, with utmost precision.
