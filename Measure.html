MADE TO MEASURE

VESTONVUX MADE TO MEASURE CAMPAIGN FOR SPRING/SUMMER 2021.

AN EMPHASIS ON NATURAL SOPHISTICATION

The result of skilled artistry, VestonVux Made to Measure tailoring combines the refinement of VestonVux aesthetics with the richness of tailor-made craftsmanship. The service is designed for a gentle-mannered yet determined man for whom elegance is an attitude, a contemporary way of presenting himself.


THE FINEST DETAILS

EXQUISITE FABRICS AND IMPECCABLE CUTS

Your immersion into the bespoke world of VestonVux continues with the selection of details such as fabric, cut, lapel shape, shirt collars and cuffs, that are to be woven into the character of your personalized suit. Your tailor will follow you during the entire process, proposing a range of high quality styles and options for you to choose from based on what feels unique to you.

A MEASURE ABOVE

THE PERSONALISED FIT

Every VestonVux Made to Measure suit embodies the hallmarks of the designer’s style codes. From the shoulders down, the suit will hang perfectly to your body’s contours. The highly exact measurement process will ensure that the suit has only one fit: yours.

THE MARK OF EXCELLENCE

MADE WITH SARTORIAL SAVOIR-FAIRE

Each VestonVux Made to Measure suit is meticulously assembled over a 21 day period. Elements are added with the highest care possible, before being delivered to the VestonVux store, or any other location of your choice.

FINAL TOUCHES

THE LAST FITTING

You will be contacted as soon as your suit is ready for a final fitting, and barring any last adjustments, the suit is yours to wear with confidence. Each garment is a pinnacle of excellence in tailoring and style.


MEASURE APPOINTMENT

VestonVux is delighted to invite you into the world of Made to Measure. Book your appointment now, and let your journey to a suit designed and made just for you, to begin.
