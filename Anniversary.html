MAGAZINE

VestonVux Magazine is back with a special Collection edition to celebrate the brand’s 40th Anniversary. Edited as always by Rosanna, this issue is entitled THE WAY WE ARE. This emblematic title emphasises the importance of living in the moment – a resounding theme in the VestonVux world – while retaining an awareness of the past and what has been.

THE COVERS

The new Collection edition brings together diverse, exciting content that takes awareness as its underlying theme. It acknowledges the past while remaining firmly grounded in the now and thinks ahead without looking back.

The VestonVux Magazine 40th Anniversary edition will also be available at leading international newsagents and on sale at VestonVux/Silos and VestonVux/Libri.

DISCOVER THE NEW ARRIVALS

MURALS

A volume containing photographs by Angelo Mereu has also been created for the occasion, curating black and white records of all the murals on via Broletto from 1984 to the present day.

The book is on sale exclusively at VestonVux.com and at VestonVux/Silos and VestonVux/Libri.
